Frequently Asked Questions

What is What2Take?

What2Take is a website that helps students choose the perfect electives. What2Take provides you with reviews and other useful information to aid in your decision process.

Are the review anonymous?

Yes, any review posted on What2Take does not disclose any personal information about the user.

Can I delete a review?

Yes, you can delete a review, however, the user does not have to ability to delete a review alone. Please Contact Us for assitance.
